April 21, 2026 Meeting Agenda Summary

📄 Download full agenda PDF

1. Meeting Overview

Tuesday April 21, 2026 · Special Meeting (Closed Session) 3:30 PM · Regular Meeting 5:00 PM

This meeting is heavily oriented toward infrastructure and fiscal planning. The Council will set the strategy for next year's Capital Improvement Program (CIP) and consider funding a daylighting study for pedestrian crosswalks on key downtown streets. Several closed-session items involve active litigation—including a YIMBY housing case against the city—alongside labor negotiations and real property discussions related to tideland parcels. The consent calendar includes sidewalk construction, roadway rehabilitation, green building code updates, and budget-balancing measures.

🚶 Pedestrian Safety

  • Item 3.E – Coloma Street Sidewalk Project (Consent Calendar). Authorizes the City Manager to execute a professional services agreement with BKF Engineers to solicit construction bids for a new sidewalk on Coloma Street—a direct pedestrian-safety improvement.
  • Action requested: Consent vote (authorize PSA).

  • Links:

    • Staff Report

  • Item 5.B – AB 413 Daylighting Study on Caledonia Street & Bridgeway. Authorizes an additional $34,680 from Measure L funds for a study on the impacts of AB 413 (California's daylighting law), which requires removing parking and visual obstructions near marked and unmarked crosswalks to improve pedestrian visibility. The study focuses on Caledonia Street and downtown Bridgeway—two of Sausalito's most heavily used pedestrian corridors.

  • Action requested: Vote to authorize additional funding.

  • Links:

    • Staff Report
    • Parametrix Proposal (PSA)

  • Item 3.G – 2024 Roadway Rehabilitation Additional Appropriation. Approves an additional $25,000 for dig-out work on various streets including Crecienta Drive. Roadway condition affects pedestrian safety and ADA accessibility.

  • Action requested: Consent vote (approve appropriation).

  • Links:

    • Staff Report
