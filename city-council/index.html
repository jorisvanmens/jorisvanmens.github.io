May 19, 2026 Meeting Agenda Summary

1. Meeting Overview

Tuesday May 19, 2026 · Special Meeting (Closed Session) 3:30 PM · Regular Meeting 5:00 PM

This meeting is dominated by city leadership transition and fiscal planning. The Council will appoint an Interim City Manager through the end of June and conduct its second review of the FY 2026–2027 preliminary General Fund budget, including analysis of tax/revenue opportunities and pension smoothing. Other notable items include reviewing proposals for the Turney Street Floating Dock improvement project, a municipal code cleanup ordinance removing outdated officer bond and city manager removal provisions, and a sea level rise adaptation engineering contract.
