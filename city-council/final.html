💬 Public Comments

Pelican Harbor Lease Assignment

16 public comments. Commenters overwhelmingly urged the City Council to withhold or condition consent to the proposed lease assignment to Cameron Razavi / Pelican Twins, LLC. The dominant concerns were the prospective buyer's code enforcement history and prior legal violations (at Bridgeway Marina and in Sacramento), the potential displacement of long-time liveaboard residents through rent increases, and the City's public trust obligation to vet any new operator of tidelands property. Nearly all commenters requested thorough due diligence, a public hearing, and enforceable protections for existing residents before any consent is granted.

Post Office Drive-Up Mailbox Accessibility

15 public comments. Residents uniformly requested that the drive-up mail collection box at the Sausalito Post Office be fitted with a "snorkel" chute so it can be used from a vehicle without exiting the car. Commenters emphasized the impact on seniors and people with disabilities who cannot safely get out of their cars to deposit mail, and asked the City Council to advocate with USPS to make this modification.
